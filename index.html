About Me

Jeweled micro-mosaics have been my specialty, since 2008. I like making distinctive jewelry pieces and small home accents that dazzle. I use premium quality jewels, beautiful Austrian-made Swarovski pointed-back rhinestones, vintage Bohemian glass cabochons, beads, chain, shells, and other rare goodies.
